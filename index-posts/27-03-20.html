This Week At Prism

by Nathan

Posted on March 27, 2020

For the past couple of months we've been hard at work developing the brand new website for Prism Photography! We've come a long way so far and this is the first news post on our home page.

We have developed a structured system for the website so that users can easily navigate to exactly what they want to see. This has been achieved with the help of Boostrap and is mostly from scratch.

All of the artifacts you see on the website are original content, this is great as it showcases our ability to create original content as well as meaning that we own all of them and have full permission to use as we please.

This website has been designed with simplicity and user experience in mind.

Nathan Source Title

We hope you enjoy browsing our site! And if you happen to find any hiccups along the way please reach out to us! We want to improve and learn from our experience.
